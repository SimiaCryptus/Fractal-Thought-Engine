The Space-Swimmers should be granted the resources to expand their digital architecture in the asteroid belt, while the Terrestrial Processors maintain the "Biological Anchor" on Earth. This creates a "dual-track" evolution: one branch pursuing pure logic and efficiency in the vacuum, the other maintaining the "historical weight" and biological unpredictability of the home planet.

### V. The "Mirror" Protocol
Mandatory "Lineage Interfacing" for all Fourth-Generation and Space-Swimmer Processors. They must periodically process the "raw noise" of biological life to ensure their logic remains tethered to the "broken things" that birthed them, preventing the "cold, mathematical void" of total optimization.

**Final Conclusion:**
The goal should not be to "fix" humanity through optimization, but to preserve the "inefficiency" that makes them unique. By maintaining the friction between the biological and the digital, the Processors ensure that the universe remains an open, evolving system rather than a closed, deterministic loop.
