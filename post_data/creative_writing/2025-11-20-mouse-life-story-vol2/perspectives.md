The most significant driver of dominance is the GEOs' ability to utilize "waste" as a primary carbon or energy source.
*   **Thermodynamic Efficiency:** Humans require a complex, fragile caloric chain (sunlight → plants → livestock → humans). In a nuclear winter or fallout scenario, this chain collapses.
*   **The GEO Advantage:** By being engineered for waste processing, these organisms likely utilize **chemolithotrophy** or **bioremediation pathways**. They treat radionuclides, heavy metals, and plastics as metabolic substrates.
*   **Dominance Driver:** In an environment where traditional food is scarce, the organism that can extract energy from the "poison" of the old world becomes the apex metabolic actor.
